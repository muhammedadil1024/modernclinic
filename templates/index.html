Our Departments

Our departments are specialized units that provide focused healthcare services to patients. They encompass a wide range of areas such as cardiology, emergency, radiology, surgery, and more. These departments collaborate to deliver comprehensive medical care, aiming to improve patient health outcomes and enhance overall well-being.

CARDIOLOGY

Specializes in diagnosing and treating heart-related conditions and diseases.

GENERAL MEDICINE

Provides comprehensive care for adults, including diagnosis, treatment, and management of various medical conditions.

SURGERY

Conducts surgical procedures to treat a range of medical conditions.

EMERGENCY

Provides immediate medical care for patients requiring urgent attention due to illness or injury.
